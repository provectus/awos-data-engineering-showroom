# Citi Bike Demand Analytics Platform - V2 Summary

## Vision

Predict bike demand based on weather conditions, weekdays/holidays, and special events to improve inventory management, increase bike availability for customers, reduce operational costs, and boost revenue.

## Target Audience

Bike-share operations teams including Operations Managers, Fleet Managers, Business Analysts, and Urban Planners who need to optimize bike distribution and rebalancing decisions.

## Core Features

- **Historical Demand Analytics** - Analyze past trip patterns, peak hours, and station utilization trends
- **Weather Impact Analysis** - Understand correlations between weather conditions and ridership demand
- **Holiday & Event Detection** - Track demand changes during holidays and special events (concerts, sports, parades)
- **Demand Forecasting** - Predict future bike demand by station, time, and conditions
- **"What-If" Predictive Scenarios** - Model hypothetical situations combining weather + events + day-of-week
- **Interactive Dashboards** - Visualize trends, heatmaps, station comparisons, and forecast accuracy

## Success Metrics

- Reduce empty/full station incidents by 40%
- Reduce rebalancing operational costs by 30%
- Increase bike utilization rate by 20%
- Achieve 85%+ demand forecast accuracy
- 90%+ bike availability during peak hours
